# Cvent Email Communication Templates

Copy these into your Cvent email builder. Replace `{{VARIABLES}}` with your event details. Merge fields in `[brackets]` are Cvent system merge fields.

---

## 1. Invitation

**Subject:** You're Invited: {{EVENT_NAME}} — {{DATES}}

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

{{ONE_SENTENCE_HOOK}}

**{{EVENT_NAME}}** takes place **{{DATES}}** at **{{VENUE}}** in **{{CITY}}**.

{{TWO_SENTENCE_VALUE_PROP}}

[Register Now Button]

{{EARLY_BIRD_LINE_IF_APPLICABLE}}

Questions? Reply to this email or contact {{CONTACT_EMAIL}}.

---

## 2. Registration Confirmation

**Subject:** You're Registered for {{EVENT_NAME}}

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

You're confirmed for **{{EVENT_NAME}}**!

**Your Details:**
- Registration type: [Registration Type]
- Sessions: [Session List]

**What's Next:**
- {{NEXT_STEP_1}}

---

## Tips

- Keep every email **under 200 words** — people skim on phones
- Subject lines under **50 characters** avoid truncation
- Avoid spam trigger words: "free," "act now," "limited time"
- Test merge fields with a real test registration — don't assume they work
- Set reply-to as a real person, not a no-reply address
