## How to Fill This Out

### Time Column
- Use exact times, not ranges: "9:00 AM" not "morning"
- Include 15-minute buffer warnings before transitions: "8:45 AM — BUFFER: Ballroom must be ready"
- Mark simultaneous events clearly: "9:00 AM [Room A + Room B + Room C]"

### Behind the Scenes
This is where the real work happens. Include:
- When AV needs to be tested
- When catering should deliver
- When staff need to be in position
- When doors open vs. when the session starts

### If Late Column
For every major block, answer: "If this runs 15 minutes late, what do we cut or compress?"

## Pre-Event Checklist (Morning Of)

- [ ] Walk the venue — are rooms set correctly?
- [ ] Test all AV in every room
- [ ] Confirm catering quantities and delivery times
- [ ] Brief volunteers on their assignments
- [ ] Confirm registration desk is set up and staffed
- [ ] Check signage is posted at all decision points (elevators, hallways, restrooms)
- [ ] Distribute printed run sheets to all key staff
- [ ] Charge your phone and backup battery

## What Can Go Wrong

| Issue | Response |
|-------|----------|
| Speaker no-show | Have backup content or extend Q&A. Know who can fill 15 minutes. |
| AV failure | Know where the backup projector is. Have presentation on USB, not just laptop. |
| Catering late | Buy time with a "networking moment" or extended break. |
| Room not ready | Have a holding area identified. Direct attendees there while setup finishes. |
| Medical emergency | Know where the AED is. Have venue security number on speed dial. |
